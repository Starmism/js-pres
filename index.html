My Recommendations

  • React is the most useful, and will get you the most jobs.
  • Vue is very nice, and used a lot over React so can be good to know.
  • Angular is great for big companies and very opinionated.
  • Svelte is still pretty new and not set-in-stone. (Svelte 5)

Metaframeworks?

Metaframeworks are a big part of choosing a framework as well.

My Recommendations:

  • React: Next.js, Remix
  • Vue: Nuxt
  • Angular: Analog
  • Svelte: SvelteKit
